05 / Motion & Animation

Motion Language

Fast-in, slow-out. Like a terminal response — instant acknowledgement, deliberate output.

Micro Feedback

Button presses, hover states, badge flickers. Instant visual response at human-interaction speed.

duration: 100–150ms
easing: ease-out

Content Reveal

Sections sliding into view on scroll. Stagger children by 50ms each for depth without jank.

duration: 400–600ms
easing: cubic-bezier(0.16, 1, 0.3, 1)

Page Transitions

Cross-fade and upward shift. Never flashy — the interface is the star, not the motion.

duration: 300ms
easing: ease-in-out

Glitch Effect

Reserved for brand moments only: hero headlines, error states, splash screens. Use sparingly.

clip-path + translate offset
Max 2× per page

Progress / Loading

Pulse on the status dot. Scan-line shimmer on loading skeletons. Always cyan.

pulse: 2s infinite
skeleton: 1.5s ease-in-out

Reduced Motion

All animations must respect prefers-reduced-motion. Strip to 0.01ms duration — no exceptions.

@media (prefers-reduced-motion)
Accessibility non-negotiable
06 / Voice & Tone

Brand Voice

Developer-native. No corporate softness. No inflated claims. Precise, technical, with a dry edge of wit.

We Are

Direct and precise — say exactly what it does
Technical without being elitist
Confident in our open-source status
Dry wit — one-liners that land, never forced
Community-first, jargon-light documentation
Numbers and benchmarks over adjectives

We're Not

Marketing-speak: "revolutionary", "paradigm-shifting"
Overly casual — no "Hey there, friend!"
Apologetic or hedge-everything cautious
Emoji-heavy in technical documentation
Vague: "powerful tool that helps you do stuff"
Dismissive of non-developer users
✓ DO write like this
"Crawl a site. Get clean markdown. Feed it to your LLM. Takes 3 lines of Python."
Clear, functional, developer-respecting. Proves value in one sentence.
✗ DON'T write like this
"Empower your AI journey with our revolutionary, next-generation web intelligence platform!"
Means nothing. Respects no one. Contributes zero information.
